# Episode 1 Production Script

## Series

Claro English

## Episode Title

First Day in the U.S.: At the Airport

## Production Goal

This version is designed for recording, editing, subtitles, and YouTube visual production.

## Runtime Target

- 8 to 12 minutes

## Cast

- Emma: lead teacher, calm and clear
- Daniel: learner-side co-host, warm and reassuring
- Officer voice: can be Emma with a slightly firmer tone

## Delivery Direction

### Emma

- Speak slowly and clearly
- Sound warm, steady, and confident
- Slightly slower during phrase explanations and repeat-after-me sections
- Never sound overly dramatic or robotic

### Daniel

- Sound natural and supportive
- Represent the listener's questions and nervousness
- Slightly more conversational than Emma
- Keep a relaxed, reassuring tone

## Visual Style Direction

- Use clear subtitles throughout
- Highlight key phrases on screen when first introduced
- Use simple scene cards to separate sections
- Keep visuals calm, clean, and beginner-friendly
- Prefer airport-themed visuals, icons, and minimal motion graphics

## On-Screen Text Style

- Large, high-contrast English phrase text
- Optional smaller Spanish helper text later if needed
- Use 1 phrase at a time during practice sections
- Keep text uncluttered
