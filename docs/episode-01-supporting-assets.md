# Episode 1 Supporting Assets

## Episode

- Series: Claro English
- Episode title: First Day in the U.S.: At the Airport
- Primary tagline: Real English for Real Life in the U.S.

## Episode Summary

In this first Claro English lesson, Emma and Daniel help beginner English learners understand simple airport English for arriving in the United States. You will hear common questions, easy answers, and polite phrases you can use if you do not understand. This episode is designed for Spanish speakers who want more confidence with real-life English from their very first day in the U.S.

## Show Notes

In this episode, you will learn:

- How to answer simple airport questions in English
- What "What is the purpose of your visit?" means
- How to talk about how long you are staying
- How to say where you are staying
- What to say if you do not understand

Key phrases from this episode:

- Here is my passport.
- I am here for vacation.
- I am here for work.
- I am visiting family.
- I am staying for two weeks.
- I am staying at a hotel in Miami.
- Can you repeat that, please?
- Sorry, I am learning English.

Practice challenge:

Pause and answer these questions out loud:

- What is the purpose of your visit?
- How long are you staying?
- Where are you staying?

Next episode:

- Getting a ride and going to your hotel or home after the airport
