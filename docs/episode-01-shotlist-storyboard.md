# Episode 1 Shot List and Storyboard

## Episode

- Series: Claro English
- Episode title: First Day in the U.S.: At the Airport
- Runtime target: 8 to 12 minutes

## Production Goal

This shot list is designed to help a video editor assemble a clean, calm, beginner-friendly episode using a mix of host visuals, text cards, airport imagery, and phrase overlays.

## Visual Principles

- Keep the screen uncluttered
- Show only one key phrase at a time during teaching moments
- Use calm transitions and avoid fast cuts
- Support the learner with visual repetition, not flashy motion
- Keep Emma and Daniel visually consistent throughout the episode

## Asset Types Needed

- Emma host portrait or talking-head visual
- Daniel host portrait or talking-head visual
- Branded intro card
- Section cards
- Phrase cards
- Airport b-roll or simple airport illustrations
- End card with CTA

## Color / Style Guidance

- Clean, bright, welcoming visual palette
- Professional and modern, but not corporate
- Light backgrounds or soft gradients
- Clear typography with strong contrast

## Editor Checklist

- Keep subtitle timing slightly longer than normal for beginners
- Use consistent lower-thirds for Emma and Daniel
- Reuse the same phrase card style across all teaching moments
- Keep transitions smooth and simple
- Keep b-roll relevant and calm
- Avoid overwhelming the learner with too many graphics at once

## Reusable Template Note

This storyboard should become the base structure for future Claro English episodes, with new scene cards and new phrase overlays for each lesson.
