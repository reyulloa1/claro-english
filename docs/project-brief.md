# Project Brief

## Core Idea

Create an AI-assisted English learning podcast for people who already speak another language and want to improve practical English through engaging, repeatable content.

The brand should feel:

- Helpful
- Easy to follow
- Culturally welcoming
- Consistent across video, audio, social, web, and app

## Primary Audience

People who:

- Speak a foreign language and want to learn or improve English
- Prefer listening and watching over textbook-style learning
- Need conversational English for work, travel, school, or daily life
- Want short practice moments between longer lessons

## Value Proposition

The podcast helps learners improve English through:

- Real conversations
- Slow and clear explanations
- Vocabulary in context
- Pronunciation support
- Repetition and recap
- Companion tools in the app and website

## Content Pillars

1. Everyday English
2. Travel and relocation English
3. Work and interview English
4. Pronunciation and listening practice
5. Common mistakes and corrections
6. Cultural context and idioms

## Content Formats

### Long-form podcast

- 20 to 45 minute video episodes for YouTube
- Same episode exported as audio for podcast platforms
- Structure should be predictable so learners know what to expect

Suggested episode pattern:

1. Topic intro
2. Real dialogue or scenario
3. Teaching breakdown
4. Vocabulary recap
5. Pronunciation focus
6. Practice prompt or homework

### Short-form content

- Instagram clips and carousels
- X posts and threads
- Optional YouTube Shorts and Reels

Short-form should be generated from each long episode, not created from scratch.

## Platform Goals

### YouTube

- Main discovery engine for video episodes
- Clips and Shorts for growth
- Clear playlists by learning level and topic

### Spotify and Apple Podcasts

- Audio-only distribution of full episodes
- Consistent show notes and episode metadata

### Instagram

- Audience engagement
- Short lessons, quotes, recap clips, vocabulary cards

### X

- Quick educational posts
- Threads from episode summaries
- Launch and update announcements

### Website

- Home base for the brand
- Episode archive
- Email capture
- Links to YouTube, podcast apps, social accounts, and app
- SEO pages for searchable English learning topics

### App

- Free learning companion
- Lightweight premium layer for monetization

## Monetization Model

### Free tier

- Access to podcast and website
- Basic lesson notes
- Limited app features

### Paid options

- One-time upgrade for a premium pack or lifetime unlock
- Monthly subscription for ongoing premium tools

Possible premium features:

- Interactive transcripts
- Vocabulary saving
- Quizzes
- Personalized lesson recommendations
- Downloadable worksheets
- Pronunciation practice
- Premium episode notes

## Automation Goal

The system should automate as much of the publishing loop as possible:

1. Generate episode outlines
2. Produce scripts and supporting assets
3. Create video and audio deliverables
4. Generate metadata, show notes, and transcripts
5. Publish or prepare drafts for each platform
6. Repurpose content into social posts
7. Sync updates to the website and app

Important note:
Fully autonomous publishing is possible for some steps, but social accounts, YouTube uploads, podcast distribution, and payment systems usually benefit from a human approval checkpoint for quality and account safety.

## Brand Principles

- Speak clearly, never condescendingly
- Teach real English, not robotic textbook phrases
- Support multiple learner levels
- Keep visuals friendly and modern
- Make every long episode feed every other channel

## Key Success Metrics

- Weekly episode output consistency
- Video watch time
- Podcast downloads
- Website email signups
- Free app activations
- Upgrade conversion rate
- Retention of paid users

## Immediate Unknowns To Define Next

- Exact learner level focus: beginner, intermediate, or mixed
- Whether the host voice is fully AI, human plus AI, or avatar-based
- Which source languages to support first
- Brand name, tone, and visual identity
- Preferred tech stack for website and app
